Polish & Proof — Website Planning Document

Site Name

Polish & Proof

This name conveys a professional proofreading and polishing service, emphasizing clarity, correctness, and refinement in documents.

Optional domain availability: polishandproof.com (sample only)

Site Purpose

“Polish & Proof” exists to provide fast, reliable, and high-quality proofreading and light editing services for students, job seekers, and small businesses. The website will:

  • Showcase proofreading and editing services with clear pricing
  • Explain the workflow — what clients submit and what they receive
  • Display testimonials and trust indicators
  • Provide an accessible contact form for requesting quotes

User Scenarios

  • “I’m a student—can someone quickly proofread my assignment before I submit it tomorrow?”
  • “How much will it cost to proofread my business proposal or resume?”

Color Scheme

Primary – #4A90E2 (Soft Professional Blue)
Used for headings, buttons, and highlights to communicate trust and professionalism.

Accent – #F5A623 (Warm Gold)
Used for call-to-action accents, icons, and subtle highlights.

Neutral – #2C3E50 (Dark Slate)
Used for body text for readable contrast.

Background – #F7F9FC (Very Light Gray)
Used for page backgrounds to create a clean and modern feel.

Typography

Poppins — Used for all headings (H1–H4). Clean and modern.

Merriweather — Used for body text. Comfortable and readable.
